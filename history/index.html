OUR HISTORY

The Thunder Warriors, emerged from the shores of Norway in the late 9th century. We were founded by the legendary chieftain Ragnar Stormcaller, who is said to have been blessed by the god of thunder, Thor. Our clan quickly gained a reputation for their electrifying raids across the northern seas. Renowned for our fierce battles and unyielding spirit, protected by armor adorned with symbols of lightning and swords forged from meteorite iron, we march in to battle, always emerging victourious. We believe that each battle is a storm summoned by our god, and that lightning and thunder only gives us more strength. As our saga spread, our clan not only conquered vast territories but also became protectors of our homeland, fostering alliances with neighboring tribes. The Thunder Warriors left an indelible mark on history and continue to do so, inspiring new generations with tales of valor, loyalty, and the unbreakable bond between warriors.
